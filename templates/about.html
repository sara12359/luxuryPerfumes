Our Heritage

Founded in the heart of Grasse in 1892, Maison de Parfum has dedicated over a century to the pursuit of olfactory perfection. Our atelier represents a sanctuary where the rarest botanical essences are transformed into liquid poetry.

Each fragrance is a testament to the artisan's touch—blended by hand, aged in dark cellars, and poured into crystal flacons. We source only the most exquisite ingredients: ethically harvested Oud from Southeast Asia, delicate Rose de Mai picked at dawn, and Iris Pallida aged for three years.

Our commitment is not merely to create scent, but to evoke emotion, memory, and profound beauty. This is the art of the connoisseur.
